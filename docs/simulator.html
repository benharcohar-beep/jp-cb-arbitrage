What to try Slide concurrent positions from 5 down to 1 — watch CAGR rise from ~24% to ~50% as concentration increases. Slide transaction cost from 60 up to 150bp — see the strategy's edge shrink dramatically (CB arb is highly sensitive to execution costs). Slide exit horizon below 30 days — too short and you cut off late convergence; too long and you bleed theta.
